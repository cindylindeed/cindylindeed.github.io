Cindy Lin

is currently interning as a Software Engineer at Coursera.

Cindy is a junior at Stanford University, majoring in Computer Science. Because she started college as a premedical student, she is interested in how healthcare intersects with technology and is concentrating in Biocomputation within the major.

She section leads for Stanford's CS 106 series, is a board member for Stanford Women in CS, is a Mandarin interpreter for Arbor Free Clinic, and will be directing Testimony Christian A Cappella for the 2016-17 school year.

Cindy spent exactly half her life in Taiwan and the other half in the Bay Area. She enjoys running the occasional half-marathon, attempts to read as much as she can, and is a fan of careful clutter.
